About me

I am Nilesh Kumar Sahu, and I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal. My research focuses on applying computer science to address real-world problems, and I am committed to conducting top-notch research that contributes positively to the field. My areas of expertise include Statistics, Data Science, and Machine Learning. In my current research, I am exploring the utilization of wearable sensors for detecting and mitigating mental disorders. This involves designing studies, collecting data, processing signals, conducting Statistical inference and Multivariate Analysis, performing Data analysis, and developing machine learning models to achieve the research objectives. I am dedicated to making a meaningful impact through my work.
