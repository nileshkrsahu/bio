Ph.D. Scholar in EECS at IISER, Bhopal

I am a 4th year Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal, India. My research focuses on Digital Healthcare and Ubiquitous Computing, with expertise in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, and Fine-tuning LLMs. I have extensive experience in designing HCI studies, collecting and analyzing data from wearable sensors and textual sources, and developing AI models for mental disorder detection and healthcare applications. My work has been published in top-tier conferences and journals, and I actively contribute to the research community through publications, conference presentations, and volunteer roles.
